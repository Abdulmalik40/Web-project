Plan Overview

Designed for travelers who want to absorb every facet of Saudi Arabia, the 30-day Grand Discovery balances spiritual reflection, cultural depth, and rejuvenating downtime. Each week focuses on a different region, with curated accommodations, guided experiences, and flexible free days to explore independently.

Duration 30 Days

Four themed weeks with optional rest days built into each stage.

Pace Balanced

Alternating immersive excursions with restorative beach and spa time.

Regions Covered Kingdom-Wide

Makkah, Madinah, Riyadh, AlUla, Red Sea coast, Asir Highlands, and NEOM.

Ideal For Deep Explorers

Families and cultural travelers seeking authentic encounters plus premium comfort.

Week-by-Week Journey

Week 1

Spiritual Origins

  • Pilgrimage-inspired stays in Makkah and Madinah
  • Guided visits to historic mosques and sacred landmarks
  • Evening reflections and traditional Hijazi dining
Week 2

Culture & Heritage

  • Diriyah living history, Riyadh skyline experiences
  • AlUla sandstone valleys, Hegra UNESCO explorations
  • Hands-on artisan workshops and farm-to-table dinners
Week 3

Coastal Horizons

  • Jeddah Corniche, Red Sea snorkeling, yacht charter options
  • NEOM & The Line preview with sustainability showcases
  • Wellness retreats and curated sunset gatherings
Week 4

Southern Highlands

  • Asir mountain villages, cloud forests, and cable car vistas
  • Jazan spice markets and Farasan Island marine sanctuary
  • Final days for bespoke interests or private desert safari
