Where to Stay

Handpicked accommodations that blend comfort, location, and authentic Saudi hospitality.

Jeddah (Days 1-3 & 10-12)

Luxury: The Jeddah EDITION

Stunning Red Sea views, rooftop pool, and world-class dining. Steps from the Corniche with private beach access.

Mid-Range: Ibis Jeddah City Center

Central location near Al-Balad, easy parking, comfortable rooms, and excellent value. Perfect for exploring historic Jeddah.

Boutique: Jeddah Hilton

Waterfront location with multiple dining options, spa facilities, and easy access to both beach and city attractions.

AlUla (Days 4-6)

Luxury: Chedi Hegra

Ultimate desert luxury with stunning canyon views, infinity pools, and direct access to heritage sites. Includes gourmet dining.

Mid-Range: Sahary AlUla Resort

Unique tent-style rooms nestled in rock canyons, private bathrooms, excellent breakfast, and exceptional staff service.

Budget-Friendly: Rural Compound AlUla

New property in town with modern amenities, great reviews, and easy access to tour departure points.

Abha (Days 7-9)

Premium: Abha Palace Hotel

Mountainside location with panoramic views, spa facilities, and traditional Asiri architecture blended with modern luxury.

Best Value: Abha Gardn Millennium Hotel

Exceptional value with luxury rooms, easy parking, helpful staff, and proximity to cable cars and heritage villages.

Boutique: Intour Hotel Abha

Modern design in the heart of Abha, close to restaurants and shopping, with comfortable rooms and mountain views.
